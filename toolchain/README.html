AVR Microcontroller Toolchain Notes

This toolchain includes binutils, gcc, and avr-libc. It may be built for Linux, OpenBSD, and Windows (using Cygwin). The Linux, OpenBSD and Windows releases are virtually identical in every respect. (This is the reason I created my own toolchain: I routinely flip back and forth among Linux, OpenBSD, and Windows development environments.)

I use the Atmel AVRISP mkII and avrdude for in system programming. Since avrdude is rather OS dependent, and native binaries are available for all of the environments I am interested in, I do not include it in my toolchain.
